Frequently Asked Questions

Questions We Get Asked Most

What types of corporate events does Brand 360 manage?

The full spectrum — conferences, product launches, annual days, award ceremonies, dealer meets, exhibitions, employee engagement, and live productions. All delivered entirely in-house across Chennai and pan-India.

Is all your production equipment in-house?

Yes. LED walls, professional sound, intelligent lighting, custom stage design and fabrication — all owned and operated by our in-house team. We don't rely on any third-party production vendors.

How many years of experience does Brand 360 have?

Over 15 years, 500+ events delivered, and a client roster spanning Fortune-affiliated corporates, FMCG leaders, and fast-growing brands across India.

Do you handle social media and digital marketing too?

Yes — and the same in-house team handles it. Social media strategy, live event coverage, reels, paid advertising, and performance analytics, all under one brief.

Which cities do you serve?

Headquartered in Chennai, we serve clients across Tamil Nadu, Bangalore, Coimbatore, Hyderabad, and pan-India for large-scale corporate events and brand activations.
